Monster Modes

A monster's current activity is reflected in its monster mode, which is one of the following.

Standing

Standing (nothing)

Doing nothing.

Standing (special)

Standing with a special animation.

Walking

Walking (ne, n or nw)

Walking either north east, north or north west.

Walking (se, s or sw)

Walking either south east, south or south west.

Walking (e or w)

Walking either east or west.

Attacking

Attacking (melee)

Attacking (melee, special)

Attacking (range)

Attacking (range, special)

Getting Hit

Dying

Fading In

Fading Out

Delaying

Charging

Stoned

Petrified.

Healing
