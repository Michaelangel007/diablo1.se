Shrines

Most shrines are available in both single player and multi player games. Shrines only present in single player or multi player have been grouped accordingly.

All shrines have a chance of appearing on any dungeon level between 1 and 16, except for Enchanted Shrines which can only appear on dungeon levels between 1 and 8.

Mysterious Shrine

Effects

  • +5 to a random attribute.
  • -1 to all other attributes.

Message

"Some are weakened as one grows strong"

Hidden Shrine

Effects

  • -10 durability to a random non-indestructible equipped item. [1][2]
  • +10 durability to all other non-indestructible equipped items. [1]
[1]: Both current and maximum durability are updated.
[2]: The lower limit of the updated durability is 1.

Message

"New strength is forged through destruction"

Magical Shrine

Effects

  • Cast Mana Shield upon the player. [1]
[1]: The spell level is 2*dtype.

Message

"While the spirit is vigilant the body thrives"

Stone Shrine

Effects

  • Recharge all staves. [1]
[1]: This include both equipped staves and staves in the backpack.

Message

"The powers of mana refocused renews"

Religious Shrine

Effects

  • Repair all items. [1]
[1]: This include both equipped items and items in the backpack.

Message

"Time cannot diminish the power of steel"

Enchanted Shrine

Effects

  • -1 spell level to a random memorized spell. [1][3]
  • +1 spell level to all other memorized spells. [2]
[1]: The lower limit of the updated spell level is 0.
[2]: The upper limit of the updated spell level is 15.
[3]: If the random spell has a spell level of 15 the spell level is decremented by 2 instead of 1.

Message

"Magic is not always what it seems to be"

Notes

Can only appear on dungeon levels between 1 and 8.

Single Player Shrines

The following shrines are only present in single player games.

Gloomy Shrine

Effects

  • +2 armor class to all armors. [1]
  • -1 maximum damage to all weapons. [1][2]
[1]: This include both equipped items and items in the backpack.
[2]: The lower limit of the updated maximum damage is the minimum damage of the weapon.

Message

"Those who defend seldom attack"

Weird Shrine

Effects

  • +1 maximum damage to all weapons. [1]
[1]: This include both equipped weapons and weapons in the backpack.

Message

"The sword of justice is swift and sharp"

Thaumaturgic Shrine

Effects

  • Close and refill all open chests on the current dungeon level. [1]
[1]: The updated chests will contain new items since their seeds are renewed.

Message

"What once was opened now is closed"
